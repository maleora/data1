Well made for the first story from this author. I did like the ending, despite it being a bit predictable. Also, the tone shifted very fast but to be expected in such a short story. Overall, fun horror read. I liked how it started and I was caught up in it pretty quickly, but the ending was confusing. It really didn't explain anything.
