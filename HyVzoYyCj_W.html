There are a lot of books of this type around. You know, the books that give thumbnail biographies of old west lawmen and outlaws; the line between the two was often quite blurred and at times totally disregarded. In at least one case, the Hot Springs shootout, lawmen shot it out with each other. I've read a lot of these books, and this... Didn't finish this one.
