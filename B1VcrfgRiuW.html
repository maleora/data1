More About The Captain from Connecticut

I thoroughly enjoyed this book. It was a happy accident that I pulled it off the shelf at the library. The world needs more heros like Josiah Peabody. Have to admit, I did skim through some of the battles when they got a bit drawn out, but overall loved the history and the time period and the sweet little romance thrown in. :) I read this before , when I was 12 or 13 years old. I think I liked it back then but not now. Even the naval battles bored me. But perhaps still a good book for a 12 year old. What fun to read a book so similar to the Hornblower novels, but in which it is OK to root for the Americans and against the British. After pulling so long for Horatio Hornblower, it felt a little treasonous to cheer on the American ship. But I managed it!
