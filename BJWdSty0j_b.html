More About The Miracle of Language

I loved this book, which is essentially an introduction to linguistics. I picked it up in Seattle for reasons i can't remember (i think i was looking for a book on chess) and read it on a bus ride back to Philly. The ride almost certainly influenced my feelings about the book but it's opening is sure to hook any skeptic. Charlton Laird... One of the best books I have ever read. He makes the study of language enjoyable. A friend of mine took courses with him while he worked on his doctorate and he says Laird was a great prof.
