More About A Closer Look

While not overly exciting, this book is pretty cool. It shows small details of nature and then "zooms out", starting with a ladybug's spot and working up to the whole garden. I could definitely use this as a guessing game for preschool storytime. I liked the concept of looking more closely at plants and animals but I didn't love this book. For one, I wished that there were more plants and animals being examined. The last page shows a whole garden but we only take a closer look at three things. The other things that bugged me was the illustrations. They are really neat art-wise... Beautiful, large pictures; cute guessing game for toddlers and preschoolers.
